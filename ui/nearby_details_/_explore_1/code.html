Attraction Details

Amer Fort

Amer Fort, also known as Amber Fort, is a majestic fort located in Amer, Rajasthan, India. It is one of the principal tourist attractions in the Jaipur area, situated high on a hill. The fort is known for its artistic Hindu style elements. With its large ramparts and series of gates and cobbled paths, the fort overlooks Maota Lake.
